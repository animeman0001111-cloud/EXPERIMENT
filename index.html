About the Artisans

For generations, stone artisans in rural India have crafted cookware and tools using traditional hand methods passed down through families.

These skilled craftspeople work in small workshops and homes, shaping stone into beautiful, functional products that have been central to Indian cooking traditions for centuries.

Today, these artisans face declining visibility, limited market access, and competition from mass-produced alternatives, threatening this invaluable cultural heritage.
